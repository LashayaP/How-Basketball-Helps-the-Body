How Basketball can improve physical health:

Theobserver.com

  • Enhances cardiovascular health by increasing heart rate and endurance.
  • Strengthens muscles through constant movement, jumps, and pivots.
  • Improves bone health with high-impact activity and weight-bearing movements.
  • Boosts coordination and balance by requiring agility and body control.
  • Burns calories and aids in weight management.
